What is JADE?


Jade is a beautiful and valuable ornamental stone known for its green coloration.
It's often used in jewelry and decorative art. There are two main types of jade: jadeite and nephrite.
Jade has cultural significance in many regions, particularly in East Asia, where it's considered a symbol of purity, wisdom, and prosperity.
